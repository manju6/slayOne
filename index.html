Dental braces are orthodontic devices used to straighten and align teeth, improving bite and overall dental health. They consist of brackets, wires, and rubber bands that apply gradual pressure. Braces help correct issues like overcrowding, misalignment, and bite problems. Treatment duration varies, typically lasting 1-3 years, depending on the severity.
